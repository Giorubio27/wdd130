Our purpose is to create once in a lifetime opportunities for our customers and help the community embrace nature in all its beauty. Our mission here at White Water Rapids is to leave our clients speechless, excited, and provide an adrenaline rush unlike anything they have done before. Remember here at W.W.R we welcome rookies too. We have some of the best guides in the business.

History

Since I was a young boy I always wanted to go on adventures for a living but it seemed so difficult to get there on my own. I didnt have the tools, experience or friends to enjoy nature like I wanted to. Desiring adventure I dedicatedmy life to giving that experience to others. With a group of friends and help from our sponsors we were able to make my dream a reality. We are now proud to knowthat White Water Rafting is a way for our customers to step out of their daily routines and live an adventure one trip at a time.
